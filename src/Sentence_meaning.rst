================================================================================
Sentence meaning
================================================================================

Sentence meaning (=signal meaning, literal meaning, conventional meaning) is
conventional nonnatural meaning.

  * Mike means by 'Snow is white' that snow is white.
  * Most words. For instance, the word "up" can mean 'happy'[1]

Formally we might say:

- "`S` nonnaturally means (timeless) by `x` (that so-and-so)"
- "`x` nonnaturally means (timeless) something (that so-and-so)"
