================================================================================
Index
================================================================================

An index is a natural sign_.

Example: lightning (thunder)
Example: smoke (fire)
Example: sneeze (head cold)
Example: hands in pocket, upright posture (nonchalance)
Example: spots of measles (measles)
Example: fat belly (prosperity)

In an index there is a direct link between a sign and its object: the two are
actually connected.[1]

An index is a sign with a direct existential connection with its object.[1]
