================================================================================
Signal
================================================================================

A signal is the physical form of a message_.[1]

A signal is any behavior which conveys information_.[2]

Signals contain information.

Example: Sound waves in the air
Example: light waves
Example: electrical impulses
Example: touchings

A signal is an action by which a speaker means some thing.[4]

A signal has signal meaning.[4]

A signal may or may not be linguistic.[4] Non-linguistic signals include
gesticulations, facial expressions, and vocal gestures.[4]
