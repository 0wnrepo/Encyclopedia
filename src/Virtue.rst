Virtue
================================================================================

**Virtue** is moral excellence. A virtue is a positive trait or quality deemed
to be morally good. The opposite of virtue is vice.

Plato
================================================================================

Plato_ recognized five virtues are:

1. Wisdom

2. Temperance (roughly, self-control)

3. Justice

4. Piety

5. Courage
