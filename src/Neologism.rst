================================================================================
Neologism
================================================================================

A neologism is a newly coined word_ or phrase_ that may be in the process of
entering common use, but has not yet been accepted into mainstream language_.

* radar (Radio Detection And Ranging)
* black hole
* meme
* grep
* taser (Thomas A. Swift's Electric Rifle)
* photoshop
* google
* aspirin
* kleenex
* frisbee
* xerox
