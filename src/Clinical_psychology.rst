================================================================================
Clinical psychology
================================================================================

Clinical psychology is a branch of psychology_ that focuses on understanding,
preventing, and relieving psychological distress or dysfunction.

Professions
================================================================================

- Psychiatry
    - Medical doctor who specializes in treating mental illness
        - Licensed to write prescriptions
- Psychology
    - Has doctoral degree in psychology
    - Not medical doctors
        - Not licensed to write prescriptions

Treatment
================================================================================

- Counseling, which focuses on specific issues and is designed to address a
  particular problem

- Psychotherapy, which which focuses on uncovering the patterns and become aware
  of their effect

  - Thinks patterns of thinking and subconscious awareness affect the way the
    person interacts with the world
